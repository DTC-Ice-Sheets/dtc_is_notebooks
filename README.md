The objective of the DTC Ice Sheets Project is to design, implement, and validate a pre-operational Digital Twin
Component (DTC) focused on Ice Sheets and Ice Shelves. This DTC integrates Earth Observation (EO) data with advanced
computational modeling and stakeholder engagement to address critical topics in ice shelf stability, Greenland ice sheet
hydrology, mass balance, and climatology.
